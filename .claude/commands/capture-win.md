Turn rough notes into durable evidence in this repo.

Use `CLAUDE.md` as the operating policy. Treat `$ARGUMENTS` as the accomplishment, incident, mentoring moment, or notes to capture.

## Workflow

1. Read `docs\tracker\current-focus.md`, `docs\tracker\impact-log.md`, and `docs\tracker\promotion-case.md`.
2. Normalize the input into a concrete entry with:
   - date
   - short title
   - theme tags
   - summary
   - why it mattered
   - follow-up or metric to watch
3. Append the new item to `docs\tracker\impact-log.md`.
4. Update `docs\tracker\current-focus.md` if the work is still active or creates a new blocker.
5. Update `docs\tracker\promotion-case.md` if the new item strengthens a review narrative.
6. Keep the writing concise and evidence-based.
